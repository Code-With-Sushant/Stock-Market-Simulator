About Us



Welcome to Stock Market Simulator, a platform dedicated to providing a realistic trading experience to users of all levels. Whether you're a seasoned investor or just starting out. Simulators are great for beginners to learn the ropes of investing, including how to research stocks, place trades, and manage a portfolio. Learn About Stock Market

  • Strategies: Explore different investment strategies and techniques.
  • Backtesting: Backtesting parameters can include the capital at risk, initial capital, commission fees, portfolio size, benchmark, and average bid-ask spread.

  • Dashboard: Discover UI and chartpatterns in stock market.
  • Portfolio: Manage the MIS and CNC i.e. holding of stocks operated by an user.
  • Equity Market: Shares, which represent ownership in a company are traded.
  • Commodity Market: Commodities such as oil, wheat, energy, cocoa etc are traded.
